Position Responsibilities.

Key responsibilities include.

Frontend development of Web Applications as part of our UX improvement initiative.
Design and implement front-end code for improved scalability and performance.
Writing well-designed, testable, and efficient code including unit tests.
Engaging in effective teamwork in a Scrum team.

Qualifications.

To be successful in this role, we’d love you to have the following.

Relevant education in Computer Science or Software Engineering.
Good grip on best practices for software development from larger software companies min. 5 years.
Solid experience with front-end development in TypeScript.
Experience working with business software development and or ERP solutions.
Good communication skills and fluency in both spoken and written English.
Interest in taking ownership of work items and taking them to completion.
Strong desire to continually improve knowledge and skills.

The following areas are also desirable.

Vue.js, Jest, NPM.
Mobile development in Xamarin.Forms, C# and Xaml.
Dev tools like Visual Studio, VSCode, GitHub, terminals, SonarQube.
